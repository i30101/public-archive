About Us
We are W. T. Woodson High School's official Science Olympiad team. Our three teams of 15 competitors attend competitions around the region, challenging teams of other schools in various science-related events. Being Woodson's most competitive and rewarding extracurricular activity, we encourage all students interested to try out.
What is Science Olympiad?
science
Events
Science Olympiad consists of 23 events that cover various topics ranging from chemistry, earth earth science, eletrical engineering, and more. As a former Woodson Scioly president said, it's like a track meet for science nerds!
draw
Preparation
There are different types of events. For study events, you make a binder or a cheat sheet. For builds, you construct a physical machine. For labs or hybrids, you study and prepare for a hands-on examination.
social_leaderboard
Competition
Schools and universities around the country hold tournaments. We've been to invitationals at the National Cathedral, Princeton, and Yale. The States tournament we attend every year is held at the University of Virginia.
Why Compete
There are countless reasons to be a part of Woodson Science Olympiad.
  • Medals: At every tournament, competitors have the opportunity to earn medals for their events. Usually, the top 6 teams are awarded a . Our top competitors for the 2023 season earned 10 medals.
  • Traveling: Science Olympiad tournamnets are a great opportunity to visit cool places, especially because renowed univerities are known to hold . Running around campus to find your next event can be a great way to tour colleges like UVA and Princeton!
  • Fun with the team: When we're not competing, we're enjoying ourselves. Team members get to enjoy eating and goofing off with each other - just ask our competitors who came to our water balloon party this year.
  • Great people: In Woodson Scioly, you can meet the smartest and most dedicated people in our school. Just check out our alumni page to see what our former competitors thought about our team and where they are now.
  • Finding your fit: Each competitor can find and compete in the event that fulfills them the most. People can choose between event formats as well - options include binder events, sheet events, labs, hybrids, and builds. Topics are broad as well, ranging from electrical engineering to chemistry to remote sensing.
